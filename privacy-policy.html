Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Madzie is designed to support emotional wellbeing through journaling. Your privacy is central to this experience. This policy explains how your information is handled.

1. Your Data Stays on Your Device

All journal entries, moods, insights, and settings are stored locally on your device. Madzie does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.

2. No Accounts, No Tracking

Madzie does not require an account. It does not use analytics, tracking technologies, or third‑party data collection tools.

3. Optional AI Image Generator

If you choose to use the optional AI Image Generator, you must provide your own API key. This key is stored securely on your device and is never transmitted to Madzie or any third party other than the provider you choose.

4. No Sharing of Personal Information

Madzie does not share, sell, or disclose your information to anyone. Your data remains yours alone.

5. Device-Level Security

Your entries are protected by your device’s built‑in security features, such as passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be published here.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns, please reach out via the Support page.
